eScience'24 will be held at the Senri Life Science Center located in Osaka Senri, Japan. Senri Life Science Center is located 2-minute walk from Senri Chuo Station, about 30 minutes from the center of Osaka, second largest city of Japan. The venue is approximately 15 minutes from both Shin-Osaka Station and Osaka International Airport (Itami Airport).

There are many shops, restaurants, and bars around Senri Chuo Station.

Senri New Town, where the Senri Life Center is located in the hills of northern Osaka and is the first large scale new town built in Japan. Parks and green spaces make up about 21% of the total new town, and it is an abundant natural environment.


You are responsible for making your own travel arrangements.
